Some Areas of CS & CE

Software Engineering

The systematic and disciplined approach to designing, developing, testing, and maintaining software applications.

Database System

A structured collection of data organized and stored in a way that allows efficient retrieval, management, and manipulation.

Cryptography

The practice of secure communication through the use of mathematical techniques and algorithms to encode information, making it unreadable to unauthorized users.

Web development

The process of creating and maintaining websites and web applications, involving design, coding, scripting, and other tasks.

Computer Graphics

The creation, manipulation, and representation of visual images and multimedia content using computer technology.

Autonomous Robotics

The field of robotics focused on creating robots capable of performing tasks and making decisions without direct human intervention.
